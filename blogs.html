Toddler Travel:4 Helpful Tips when Traveling with your Toddler.

Traveling with your Toddler

Terrified to travel with your toddler on your next voyage? Well, for me the airport was probably the most stressful part of traveling. Adding a toddler into the equation, double the stress. While most people think taking a carry-on and a charged phone is all you need. Us parents need to triple the supplies for traveling with toddlers.

We have flown 3 times with our daughter and drove an 8 hour car ride to Disney World. I am not an expert at traveling with toddlers, but we have been more successful than we thought.

Learn from our mistakes and check out these tips that helped.
